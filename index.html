2 Before You Begin

Before attempting to log in, make sure you have access to the following:

  • Email Account: Access to the email address you used to register with Coinbase
  • Phone Number: The phone number linked to your account (if you remember it)
  • Government ID: Driver's license, passport, or other official identification
